# Employee Data Analysis Questions

Here are 20 questions to help you practice your data analysis skills using the `employees.csv` dataset.

## Basic Data Inspection
1. Load the `employees.csv` file into a DataFrame and display the first 5 rows.
2. How many rows and columns are in the dataset?
3. What are the data types of each column?
4. Are there any missing values in the dataset?
5. What is the average salary of all employees?

## Filtering and Selection
6. Filter the data to show only employees in the 'Engineering' department.
7. Display the names (First and Last) of employees who earn more than $100,000.
8. Find all employees who joined after January 1, 2020.
9. Who is the oldest employee in the company?
10. List all employees who work in 'San Francisco'.

## Grouping and Aggregation
11. What is the average salary per department?
12. How many employees are there in each city?
13. What is the maximum performance score in the 'Sales' department?
14. Calculate the total salary expenditure for the 'Marketing' department.
15. What is the average age of employees in each department?

## Advanced Analysis
16. Create a new column called 'Seniority' that calculates the number of years each employee has been with the company (assuming current year is 2024).
17. Who are the top 5 highest-paid employees?
18. Which department has the highest average performance score?
19. Find the employee with the highest salary in the 'Finance' department.
20. Calculate the correlation between Age and Salary.
